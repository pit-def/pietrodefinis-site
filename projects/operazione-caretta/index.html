Communication 2025 Ionian Coast, Italy

Operazione Caretta

Marine conservation Visual storytelling Citizen engagement

A storytelling sprint supporting volunteers who rescue loggerhead turtles, delivering the tools they needed to rally citizens, donors and institutions in a single nesting season.

Overview

Operazione Caretta connects rescue centres, fishermen and local governments to accelerate sea turtle protection. I embedded with the volunteer network, captured their operations in the field and crafted a content system that made complex conservation work easy to understand and support.

Highlights

  • Delivered a photo and video library tailored for social, press kits and fundraising decks.
  • Mapped community allies and designed outreach templates that empowered volunteers to run their own micro-campaigns.
  • Produced an impact narrative that helped the organisation secure partnerships with schools and coastal municipalities.
  • Hosted a citizen-science workshop showing beachgoers how to report hatchlings and turtle tracks.
