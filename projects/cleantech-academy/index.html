Cleantech Academy

Circular design Climate tech Story coaching

Sprint-based communication support for bioenergy founders turning textile waste into power, designed to help them align science, storytelling and fundraising.

Overview

Cleantech Academy is a program that accelerates impact-driven startups. I partnered with the cohort working on textile waste pyrolysis, translating their lab results into narratives that resonate with regulators, customers and investors.

Highlights

  • Designed communication canvases that unify scientific metrics, operations roadmaps and climate commitments.
  • Facilitated circular design labs to help founders map value creation from textile collection to energy deployment.
  • Coached teams through live pitch rehearsals, building a storytelling toolkit they can reuse with investors and partners.
  • Delivered a resource pack of pitch decks, copy frameworks and FAQ libraries for ongoing outreach.
